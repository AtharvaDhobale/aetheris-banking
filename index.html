Core Platform Functionality

Designed to replicate true private banking workflows and cash audit procedures

Maker-Checker Queue

Enforce strict internal verification. Tellers and clerks draft payables, receivables, or vouchers while managers approve them to post onto the ledger.

Balanced Double Entry

Real-time chart of accounts registry. Mandatory balanced debits and credits on every journal voucher to ensure error-free trial balance outputs.

Cashier vault desk

Tellers process deposits and withdrawals, perform cash handovers, and audit vault totals with automated note denomination calculators.

FD & RD Management

Log fixed deposits and recurring deposits, calculate compound maturity amounts automatically, and trigger payout updates.

Standing Instructions

Deploy weekly or monthly auto-debit transfers for regular vendor billing cycles or corporate utility expenses.

Credit & Loan Facilities

Issue business credit facilities and term loans, and dynamically render declining balance amortization installment calendars.

System Architecture

Backend Core: Java 17 + Spring Boot 3.2 + Spring Security (Stateless MVC).
Storage Ledger: In-memory H2 Database for immediate local testing with data seeding.
UI Components: Thymeleaf Server-Side templates styled with corporate white/blue layouts.

Compliance & Security

AML Controls: Suspicious high-value transfers flag automatically for manual compliance overrides.
Immutable Audit Trails: Every operator activity, browser configuration, and IP address is permanently logged.
Zero-Trust Roles: Administrators, CA Auditors, and Account Clerks have compartmentalized authority bounds.
